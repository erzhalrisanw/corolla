The Toyota Corolla

Introduced
In 1966

The first Corolla generation was introduced in November 1966 with the
new 1100 cc K pushrod engine. The Corolla Sprinter was introduced as
the fastback version in 1968, and exclusive to a Toyota Japan dealership
retail outlet called Toyota Auto Store.
